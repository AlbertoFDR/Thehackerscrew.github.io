CALL FOR SPONSORS

CrewCTF will start on April 15th at 17:00 GMT and run for 48 hours, ending on April 17th at 17:00 GMT. With beginner to expert level challenges, our contest will be a great opportunity for students (and professionals) of any skill level to pick up, practice, and master skills in cybersecurity. We provides safe, hands-on environments for contestants to develop exploits with the containerization of each challenge.

As one of the few international teams in the top 25, our team greatly values and promotes diversity. Thus, we aim to host our CTF free and open to anyone with an internet connection.

However, this would not be possible without the help of companies/sponsors like you! Sponsor dollars help us host our competition free and open for everyone!

As a sponsor, you get to build brand visibility among hundreds and potentially thousands of cyber-oriented individuals from around the world. As a part of our CTF, you will support and promote:

 1. Ethical Hacking
 2. Cybersecurity Education
 3. Diversity in the Tech and Cybersecurity Industry

Further Details: Sponsor logos and links will be displayed on the front page of our CTF website. Sponsors will be listed on our CTFtime event page and in the CrewCTF Discord server as well. We will also make dedicated flyers and Twitter posts for each sponsor.We hope that we can count on your help to make our first CTF a success!

If you are interested in sponsoring CrewCTF 2022, please reach out to thehackerscrew@protonmail.com.

The Official Website for CrewCTF 2022: https://crewc.tf/
