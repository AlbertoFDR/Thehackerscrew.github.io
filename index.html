About Us

TheHackersCrew is an international CTF team formed in 2020. The team consists of players from around the globe, featuring high school students, college students, and professionals.

Since its inception, TheHackersCrew has won 5 CTFs and placed within the top ten in a dozen others. The team currently ranks in the top 25 on CTFtime out of 20,000+ teams from around the world.
